Delete Your PlayPBNow Account

PlayPBNow (com.mcallpl.playpbnow) — how to permanently delete your account and associated data.

Delete from within the app (fastest)

  1. Open the PlayPBNow app and sign in.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email us from the address on your account and we will delete it for you:

Email PSInfo@PeopleStar.com with the subject "PlayPBNow Account Deletion Request." We verify ownership and delete the account within 30 days (typically much sooner).

What is deleted

  • Your account and login credentials (email/phone, password).
  • Your profile, player records, match history, and any match photos you shared.
  • Your beacons, lobbies, chat messages, and invites.
  • Subscription records are removed from our systems; billing history retained only as legally required by the app store / payment processor.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
