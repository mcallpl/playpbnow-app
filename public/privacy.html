PlayPBNow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

PlayPBNow ("App", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile and web application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, password, phone number, name, and profile information you create when registering
  • Match Information: Details about pickleball matches you create, including court name, address, date, time, player count, and match details
  • Player Ratings: Performance statistics, match history, wins, losses, and player skill levels
  • Communication: Messages, chat content, and invitations sent through the App

2.2 Location Data

PlayPBNow requests access to your device's precise location (GPS) to:

  • Display nearby pickleball courts and beacons within your area
  • Show matches happening near your location
  • Improve location-based features and recommendations

Location is only collected when you explicitly grant permission and while using the App. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. Location data is NOT shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

2.3 SMS Communication

PlayPBNow uses SMS for:

  • Account verification and password recovery
  • Match invitations (when you purchase SMS credits and send invites)

You must explicitly consent before we send SMS invitations to players. Each SMS invite requires:

  1. Your affirmative action (checkbox confirmation)
  2. Sufficient SMS credits in your account
  3. Valid recipient phone numbers

SMS services are provided by Twilio, Inc. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out of future messages. Standard SMS rates may apply to recipient carriers.

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, app version
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent in-app, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • IP Address: For security and fraud prevention purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain core App functionality
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process match invitations and player communications
  • Display location-based features (courts, beacons, nearby matches)
  • Improve App performance, stability, and user experience
  • Send administrative notifications (password resets, account updates)
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

PlayPBNow retains your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Account information is deleted from our systems
  • Match records and player statistics may be anonymized and retained for app improvement
  • SMS credits are forfeited (non-refundable)
  • Historical data from third-party services (like Stripe payment records) may be retained as required by law

You can request complete data deletion by contacting us at mcallpl@gmail.com.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Twilio, Inc.: For SMS verification and match invitations (SMS only, no personal data sharing)
  • Stripe, Inc.: For processing SMS credit purchases (payment processor only)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or to protect user safety

6. User Rights & Data Access

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Export: Request data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at mcallpl@gmail.com.

7. Account Deletion

PlayPBNow provides an in-app account deletion feature in the Help section. To delete your account:

  1. Open the PlayPBNow App
  2. Navigate to the Help & Directions tab
  3. Select "Delete My Account" from the Privacy & Legal section
  4. Confirm your password
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

8. Security

PlayPBNow implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password storage with hashing algorithms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data

While we use encryption and security protocols, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

PlayPBNow is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. iOS App Store Requirements

This App complies with Apple Inc.'s App Store Review Guidelines regarding privacy:

  • Explicit user consent for location data collection
  • Clear SMS consent before sending invitations
  • Transparent data retention and deletion policies
  • No undisclosed data sharing with third parties
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Clear error handling and user experience

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
