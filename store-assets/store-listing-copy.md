## Full description (4000 max)
**Play pickleball NOW.** PlayPBNow is the fastest way to get on a court — drop a beacon, fill your game in minutes, and keep score in real time.

Whether you're a coordinator packing a competitive session or a player looking for a casual foursome, PlayPBNow connects you with people ready to play near you, right now.

**📍 Location beacons**
Broadcast an open-play beacon at your court and let nearby players find and join. See live beacons around you on the map, check how far they are, and jump in. No group texts, no waiting.

**🏓 Live match scoring**
Score games in real time with a scoreboard built for pickleball. Track rounds, rotations, and results — everyone stays in sync.

**✉️ Match invites**
Invite players directly, manage who's in, and handle last-minute replacements so your game never falls apart.

**👥 Player profiles**
Build your player profile, track your matches, and connect with the players you love to play with.

**Why players love PlayPBNow**
• Fill a court in minutes, not hours
• Real-time, location-based open play
• Simple, fast scoring during your games
• Built by pickleball players, for pickleball players
