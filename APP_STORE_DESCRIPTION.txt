DESCRIPTION
================================================================================
PlayPBNow is the essential app for pickleball players who want to keep score,
track statistics, and compete with friends.

KEY FEATURES:

• Quick Match Scoring
  Create matches in under 30 seconds and tap to record scores in real-time.
  No complicated setup needed.

• Live Leaderboards
  See your ranking and your friends' standings instantly. Track wins, losses,
  and win percentage across all your matches.

• Group Management
  Create teams, invite friends, and track group statistics. Build your
  pickleball community and compete together.

• Player Invites
  Browse a network of pickleball players and send invites to grow your
  playing circle. Find new opponents and expand your game.

• Head-to-Head Statistics
  Compare detailed stats with other players. See match history, win records,
  and competitive matchups.

• Beacons
  Broadcast that you're looking to play. Nearby players get notified of your
  availability for spontaneous games.

• Broadcasts
  Share live match updates with friends in real-time. Keep your network
  informed about current games.
